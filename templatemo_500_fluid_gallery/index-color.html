  • About Me

    Hi! My name is Kazandra Salvador. I'm just a senior high student that loves to take pictures.

    About my hobby

    I started taking pictures when I received a cellphone with a decent camera as a gift. I never took it seriously until I realized that it wasn't that bad.

    Photography is the only peaceful thing that I can do besides reading. It helps me focus and it changes the way I think.

    About the Photos

    Most of the photos are sceneries, sunsets, and buildings because it is more pleasant to look at and there are wonderful places in the world that deserve recognition.

    About the Quotes

    The quotes that I used for my gallery are from my favorite actors/actresses and singers. They inspire me a lot and I believe that their quotes made the gallery more relatable.

    About the Site

    ROMANTI is derived from the word, romanticism which is a movement in the arts and literature that originated in the late 8th Century, emphasizing inspiration, subjectivity, and the primacy of the individual; beautifying the ugly
